Mixology Services for Your Special Events

Our goal is simple: serve amazing drinks and deliver an awesome experience.

We love bringing great cocktails to your gatherings! Whether you're celebrating something special or just getting together with friends, we'll work with you to make sure your guests have a fantastic time.

We keep things personal—our sweet spot is events with up to 30 guests. From planning your drink menu to the final pour, we'll chat with you about what you like, work with any dietary needs, and put together a drink lineup that fits your vibe.

We bring a mix of classic cocktail know-how and creative touches, using quality ingredients and recipes inspired by speakeasy bars. But really, we're just here to help create those moments when everyone's enjoying great drinks and good company.

What We Offer

Menu Planning & Consultation

  • Chat with us (in-person or video call) about your event and what you're thinking
  • We'll design a custom cocktail menu that works for your guests
  • Help you pick recipes or create new ones
  • Come by our home bar to taste-test the drinks
  • Get a shopping list if you want to grab the ingredients yourself
  • Pricing: One-time fee per event

Mixology Services

  • We'll bartend and mix drinks at your place of choice
  • We bring all the cocktail equipment and bar tools
  • We'll set up and clean up the bar area
  • Garnishes and presentation supplies included
  • You provide the space, tables, glasses, and ingredients
  • Pricing: Per hour
Good to Know
  • Where we serve: We currently serve in NY and NJ.
  • Perfect for: Birthdays, anniversaries, engagements, corporate events, holiday gatherings—you name it.
  • Group size: We focus on events up to 30 guests so we can give everyone great service.
  • Special needs: We're happy to work with dietary restrictions.
