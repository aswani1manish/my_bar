Mixology Services for Your Special Events

Our goal is simple: serve amazing drinks and deliver an awesome experience.

At Neighborhood Sips, we specialize in bringing professional mixology services to your intimate gatherings. Whether you're hosting a milestone celebration, a corporate event, or a casual get-together with friends, we'll collaborate with you to create a memorable cocktail experience that your guests will love.

From initial menu planning to the final pour, we work closely with you to understand your preferences, accommodate dietary restrictions, and craft a drink menu that perfectly complements your event.
