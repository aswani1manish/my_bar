Our Story

Neighborhood Sips is your go-to destination for discovering and crafting exceptional cocktails. We believe that every neighborhood deserves a place where friends can gather, share stories, and enjoy expertly crafted drinks. Our platform brings the art of mixology to your fingertips, offering a curated collection of recipes that range from timeless classics to innovative contemporary creations. Whether you're hosting a party or unwinding after a long day, we're here to help you create memorable moments, one sip at a time.

Meet your mixologist - Manish Aswani

For me, cocktails are about more than what’s in the glass — they’re about the moments we share around them. A well-made drink has the power to break the ice, bring people together, and turn gatherings into memorable experiences.

I’m deeply passionate about the craft of cocktail making and have spent years refining recipes inspired by classic speakeasy bars and premium mixology programs. My style blends precise technique, thoughtful balance, and layered flavor profiles, resulting in drinks that feel bold and expressive.

With Neighborhood Sips, my idea is simple: speakeasy-quality cocktails are no longer hidden behind secret doors — they’re right here. So step into the neighborhood. Take a sip. Make a memory.
